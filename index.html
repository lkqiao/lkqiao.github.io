About Me

I'm an EE major at Stanford passionate about circuit design, PCBs/ICs, signal processing, physics, and music. Excited about building things and solving cool problems. Especially interested in analog/RF engineering, communication systems, silicon design, sensing and control, robotics, and music tech.

I'm currently a R&D intern in the III-V Microelectronics group at Sandia National Laboratories. Previously worked on design verification at Apple and power electronics optimization of a CubeSat at Stanford.

You can reach me at lkqiao@stanford.edu.
